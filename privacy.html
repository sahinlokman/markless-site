Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Introduction

Markless ("we", "our", or "the app") is an AI-powered watermark removal application for iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Key Principle: Markless operates entirely on your device for its core features. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal photos.

Information We Collect

Our core image processing functions without collecting personal data. However, for specific app functionality, the following applies:

  • In-App Purchases: Information related to your purchases and subscription status is securely managed through Apple's App Store and our third-party provider, RevenueCat. This includes your purchase history but does not include sensitive financial data or personal identity details.
  • No photos or images are uploaded to any server; they are processed entirely on-device.
  • No personal information (name, email, etc.) is directly collected by us.

Image Processing

Markless uses on-device AI models for watermark detection and removal:

  • SigLIP2 — Neural network for watermark detection
  • U2-Net — Image segmentation for watermark masking
  • LaMa — Large Mask Inpainting for watermark removal

All AI processing happens entirely on your device. Your photos never leave your phone. No internet connection is required for image processing.

Photo Library Access

Markless requests access to your photo library to allow you to select images for processing and save processed results. This access is:

  • Only used when you explicitly select or save photos
  • Never used for background scanning or data collection
  • Managed entirely by iOS privacy controls

Data Security

All image data processing occurs entirely on your device. Subscription and purchase data is securely processed through Apple and our trusted third-party provider, RevenueCat. We employ no proprietary servers, databases, or cloud infrastructure for user photos.

Third-Party Services

Markless utilizes limited third-party services specifically for core app functionality:

  • RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat to process and manage in-app purchases and subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect certain information such as purchase history, an anonymous user ID, and basic device information to function correctly. This data is not linked to your identity or personal information. For more details, please review their Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect personal data from users, including children under 13, and is safe for users of all ages regarding its core, offline image processing functions.

Your Rights

Since we do not store your personal data on our servers, there is no direct data to access, modify, or delete through us. You have full control over all content on your device at all times. For data handled by third parties (such as Apple or RevenueCat), their respective privacy policies and data rights apply.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
